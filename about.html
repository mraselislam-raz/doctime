DocTime is an online medical service App, targeting the buoyant telehealth and e-pharmacy market. We are primarily focused on large developing markets going through a rapid digitalisation of their societal and healthcare model. Our mission is to improve the health and wellbeing of the populations we serve, with an even greater access for rural areas. We have developed advanced and affordable telehealth services that benefit both the patients and the existing public health and private medical stakeholders.

DocTime enables patients to access instantly video consultations with doctors 24/7, providing instant access to professional medical advice and online prescriptions. We verify every doctor to make sure only authorised doctors are providing consultations and enables patients to keep their previous consultation history and view online prescriptions. DocTime is vertically integrated with payment providers and pharmacies for an end-to-end digital health experience. And we promise to improve our solutions continuously through our scalable technology and data analytics capabilities.

Doctime systematically focuses on expanding access to affordable healthcare services to all. Our passionate team is committed to excellence and work extremely hard to ensure we make a positive contribution to the healthcare of the future.
