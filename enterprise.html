Why employers choose DocTime?

DocTime is an online medical service App with the aim of expanding access to affordable healthcare services. DocTime enables patients to access video consultations with doctors 24/7, providing instant access to professional medical advice and prescriptions, and other health related services.

We have launched our enterprises offering for employers to provide health benefits to their employees.

DocTime verifies every doctor to make sure only authorised doctors are providing consultations and enables patients to keep their previous consultation history and view online prescriptions.

DocTime ambitions to become the champion of e-health in the workplace. With the objective to contribute to health and happiness of all employees in the country.
